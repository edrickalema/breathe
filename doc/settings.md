Choice between enforcement levels when apps are blocked during focus sessions:
Friendly Reminder: Show overlay with companion when attempting to open blocked app. User can dismiss after reading message. Logs attempt but doesn't prevent access. Least restrictive.

Timed Delay: Show intervention overlay requiring 10-second wait before access granted. Breathing exercise during delay. Creates friction without hard blocking. Default option balancing effectiveness and autonomy.

Hard Block: Cannot access app at all during focus session. Requires ending session early to gain access. Overlay shows time remaining in session and encouragement to stay focused. Most restrictive.
